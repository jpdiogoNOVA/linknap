What is LinkNap?

Picture this: you’re on the couch or bed watching Netflix, HBO or any other streaming platform and you fall asleep, but the show keeps playing and you wind up not knowing at which minute you actually stopped watching. As you wake up the next day you find out that 2 or even 3 episodes have been playing while you were asleep... Does that sound familiar? What if there was a way to control that?


With LinkNap you can synchronize a multimedia platform with a mobile device through our app, in order to interrupt the stream as soon as you begin to fall asleep, preventing the playback from continuing and saving the exact moment he or she stopped watching.
